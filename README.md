# Zomasta — Reels Based Food Discovery Platform

**Zomasta** is a modern food discovery platform inspired by **Zomato** and **Instagram Reels**, designed to help users explore restaurants and dishes through engaging short-form videos.

Instead of browsing static restaurant listings, users can scroll through a vertical reel feed where food partners upload short videos showcasing their dishes, ambience, and specials. This approach makes food discovery more interactive, visual, and engaging.

The platform combines **social media style interaction with food marketplace functionality**, allowing users to like, save, and explore restaurant content while enabling food partners to promote their offerings through short videos.

---

## Key Features

* Vertical **Reels-style food video feed**
* **Like and Save functionality** for reels
* **Food partner dashboard** to upload food videos
* **Authentication system** for users and partners
* **Responsive mobile-first UI**
* Smooth scrolling reel playback experience
* Restaurant profile pages

## Inspiration

This project merges the discovery model of **Zomato** with the engagement model of **Instagram Reels**, creating a platform where food exploration happens through short video experiences.

---

## Project Goal

The goal of this project is to explore **modern full-stack architecture and social media interaction patterns**, including video feeds, scalable UI components, and real-world authentication systems.
